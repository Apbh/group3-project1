About Us

In a world where people are used to completing only transactions with a click of the mouse, we found that most of the major global travel technology companies didn't offer cryptocurrency as a method of payment.
Bitcoin transactions are becoming more popular these days, especially amongst travellers. Regular wire transfers and foreign purchases may often involve fees and exchange costs. Since BTC transactions have no government involvement, for example, transaction costs are kept as low as possible. Travellers enjoy the convenience of BTC transactions as transfers occur quickly while also eliminating the hassle of authorization requirements and long wait periods.
We, as a group, decided to dig deeper into this space to make it more convenient for the many people around the globe who would like this method of payment be available. Through our travel app, you get to choose your ideal hotel in whatever city while being offered the ability to view their corresponding BTC rates. This live data is a quick and easy way to decide what's best for you.
